Recipe for lasagna

Back to main page
  1. cook meat - cook in skillet till brown
  2. cook the noodles - cook in lightly salted water till al dente
  3. Make cheese layer - mix all your cheeses, eggs, seasonsings in bowl
  4. bake the lasagna - assemble everything then cook in oven covered in foil overtop

Specifications on how to cook lasagna

In an oven preheated to 375 degrees F(180 degrees celsius) should be cooked for 40-50 min

Ingredients

  • Meat: 0.5 ground pork; 0.5 ground beef
  • onion - diced
  • canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs - 2 tbs parsley; one crushed garlic clove
  • sugar - dash
  • spices and seasonings - use any spices to your hearts content
  • Noodles - use lasagna noodles - oven ready saves time
  • cheese - lots of cheese
  • Eggs - makes cheese layer extra creamy
